About

I was raised in wild Siberia above the polar circle and remember how it was exciting to see polar lights on dark winter days. This phenomenon is a great example of one of the natural solutions to provide light to polar areas. Inspiring, elegant and functional.

I am passionate to create similar pleasingly ingenious and simple experiences in digital products.

Experience
  • 25+ years of experience in understanding humans
  • 15+ years of building digital interfaces
  • 7+ years of getting paid for what I love to do
Me as a professional

Following user-centered design principles, from usability basics and typography to complex psychological tricks. I am passionate to define the entire experience, not only interface interactions but also what happens before and after that.

Qualitative research analysis and quantitative data collecting and understanding are essential parts of my design process. First solve user's needs, then think about business goals.

Expert knowledge of HTML and CSS, proficiency in JavaScript which leads to rapid prototyping and ability to design idea from visual and interaction perspective.

Two degrees, in Computer Science (Russia) and Industrial Design (The Netherlands), is a perfect combination to solve user problems and create interfaces that looking and working as fine as real products.

Experience in developing and applying design systems in digital products. From a grid system to modules and components.

Experience in teaching in Saint-Petersburg Institute of Technology (3D modeling courses) and Eindhoven University of Technology (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for Designers) opened my interest in sharing my vision with others.
