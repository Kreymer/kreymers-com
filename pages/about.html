About

I was raised in wild Siberia above the polar circle and remember how I was excited to see polar lights at dark winter days. This phenomenon is a great example of one of the natural solutions to provide light to polar areas. Inspiring, elegant and functional.

I am passionate to create similar pleasingly ingenious and simple experiences in digital products.

Experience

Me as a professional

Following user-centered design principles, from usability basics and typography to complex psychological tricks. I am passionate to define entire experience, not only interface interactions but also what happens before and after that.

Qualitative research analisys and quantitative data collecting and understanding.

Expert knowledge of HTML and CSS, proficiency in JavaScript which lead to rapid prototyping and ability to design idea from visual and interaction perspective.

Two degrees, in Computer Science (Russia) and Industrial Design (The Netherlands) is a perfect combination to solve user problems and create interfaces that looking and working as fine as real products.

Teaching in Saint-Petersburg Institute of Technology (3D modelling courses) and Eindhoven University of Technology (HTML, CSS and JavaScript for Designers) opened my interest in sharing my vision with others.

Experience in developing and applying design system in digital products. From grid system to modules and components.
