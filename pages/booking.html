Booking.com

I am currently UX Designer at Booking.com. In the last couple of years I worked on Destination Finder, Travel guides and join.booking.com.

When

2013 — Present

A/B testing is the core method to validate changes on the website at Booking.com. Taking into account the amount of people visiting the website everyday you can validate hypothesis fast. Quantitative data can tell a lot about user behaviour and how certain change affects it.

However qualitative research was always present at Booking.com. I am always actively involved in user-testing and developing and validating concepts with users. Because of non-discolsure agreement I can not share the details of work I have done but I can point the areas.

Achievements and challenges

  • Working in the team enabling properties acquisition efforts based on demand. Bringing more than X% new properties year over the year.
  • Working closely with researchers and data scientists. Solving complex user problems and achieving concrete business goals.
  • Designing for millions of users.
