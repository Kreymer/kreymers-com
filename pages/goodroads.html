Goodroads

Goodroads is a mobile app for Android and iOS devices measuring road quality while user driving.

Summary

Goodroads was started as a hackathon project by a group of friends and grew to a working and well-known product.

My role

I was part of this startup which we started with a group of friends. I was the only designer in the team and was responsible for how the app and website look and work.

The problem

Quality of roads is one of the biggest issues in Russia. Every year ocean of money is spent for roads repairing. Thousands of road accidents occur by the reason of potholes and bad roads. Goodroads provides data that could save money and even lives. Basically, the app was made for drivers who are interested in improving roads or just for people who want to know how to drive with a better road.

One more option in your auto

Goodroads is the cutting edge way of monitoring road surface condition. The mobile app installed on the Android or iOS device collects the data and sends it to the data mining server which processes data and gives it to the web server visualizing it for the user. Unlike existing methods – expert evaluation and aerial inspection, our method is rather cheap and well-rounded.

How it works

The app collects data about the road surface condition using the built-in accelerometer. Any bump gives the appropriate data and the device sends it to the server. On the server side, the pattern classification algorithm recognizes the potholes and shows the user potholes on the nearest roads.

Afterward, we use this data to build road surface map and help drivers navigate safer.

Recognition

The idea of measuring road quality by using a mobile device like tablet or smartphone and built-in accelerometer was completely new (in 2011-2012 at least). Goodroads was noticed by such companies like Google, Yandex, Opera and participated in a lot of competitions and conferences.

  • Opera Satartup Awards (2011). Took the first prize as the best startup, a mobile mapping service that collects data about road surface quality.
  • Start UP Sauna Awards (2011). Took the first place as the most promising project.
  • Slush conference participant (2011)
  • Grand prix Start Up Tour (2011))
  • WebReady Spb finalist (2011)
  • Hannover Messe participant (2013)
