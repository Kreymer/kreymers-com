What motivated us

We are passionate about health technologies and believe that this industry could be much further ahead than where it is now. We look for like-minded people and experts in the medical field. Only together can we disrupt the health industry.

Visiting a doctor is still a one-time thing happening from time to time depending on the state of health of a patient. A potential patient is someone who identifies symptoms as something needing treatment. And the patient is a key role in the process because he or she initiates the process of checking health, followed by creating an appointment, meeting a doctor, diagnosing, treatment if needed, waiting, etc.
