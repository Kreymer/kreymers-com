With LinesUp it will be easier to find new business or hobby connections based on your location. If you have long transfer connection in an airport or visiting a conference and want to meet new people this app supposed to help you to find right people.

Design process

When I started this project clients had a clear vision for the app but I suggested to do a research and understand the problem and needs of our users. We identified 3 major steps of the process we followed: Discover, Design and Deliver.

Discover state
  • Identifying a target user or group of users. Who is our user and why?
  • Task analysis. What do our users do? Explore what kind of problems do our users have (including use-cases and scenarios)?
  • Setting requirements. What should we do for our users? Based on the previous analysis set a list of requirements for our service. This list will be the basis for our design.
  • Definition of our goal. What do we do and which needs do we cover? Prioritize requirements (must, should, could, would) and by selecting the most important ones (or all of them) deliver clearly identifying task for the design.
  • Planning the design process. How are we going to design? Lean design (build – measure – learn).

Identifying personas

To understand our user group we interviewed several people and based on their portraits built main characters.

Design state
  • Conceptualization / low level visualization. How to find where to dig? First time we translate requirements into interface solutions. We come up with several concepts to define a starting point for design.
  • Team evaluation. What to select? Evaluation of the concepts and selecting/combining features.
  • Defining screens / middle level visualization. Mapping user flows with screens for mobile and web.
  • Team evaluation / user testing. Evaluate the design, test with basic scenarious and apply changes if needed.
  • Graphic and interaction design. Pixel perfect adjustments using material design.
