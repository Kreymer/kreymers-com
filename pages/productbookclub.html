We pick a new book every month

The new book will be chosen by community vote 2 months before the time for discussion.

Enjoy the reading at your own pace

We aim to discuss the whole book, but it is OK if you haven't finished it by the date of the discussion.

The idea is to discuss main takeaways, related experiences, and share some learnings. You can also just join to listen to what others think.
